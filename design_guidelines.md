# Adam's Club Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach** - Drawing inspiration from Airbnb's outdoor/experience focus and Linear's clean productivity interface. This outdoor gear rental platform requires visual appeal to showcase adventures while maintaining utility for complex booking workflows.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors (Dark Mode):**
- Primary Brand: 142 69% 58% (Adventure teal-green)
- Secondary: 25 95% 53% (Warm sunrise orange)
- Background: 240 10% 3.9% (Rich dark)
- Surface: 240 10% 8% (Elevated dark surface)

**Primary Colors (Light Mode):**
- Primary Brand: 142 76% 36% (Deep forest green)
- Secondary: 25 95% 53% (Vibrant orange accent)
- Background: 0 0% 100% (Clean white)
- Surface: 210 40% 98% (Soft gray surface)

### B. Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter via Google Fonts
- **Display Font:** Poppins for hero sections and branding
- **Hierarchy:** Bold headings (600-700), regular body (400), medium labels (500)

### C. Layout System
**Tailwind Spacing Units:** Consistent use of 2, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 24 units
- Tight spacing: p-2, gap-2
- Standard spacing: p-4, m-6, gap-4
- Section spacing: py-8, my-12
- Large sections: py-16, my-24

### D. Component Library

**Navigation:**
- Mobile-first tab bar with Home, Explore, Bookings, Wallet, Profile
- Sticky header with clean logo and minimal navigation
- Staff mode toggle in profile with distinct visual treatment

**Cards & Content:**
- Gear cards with rounded corners (rounded-lg), subtle shadows
- Experience cards with full-width imagery and overlay text
- Pricing cards with clear tier differentiation and popular badges

**Forms & Actions:**
- Primary CTA buttons with adventure teal background
- Secondary buttons with outline treatment and blurred backgrounds when over images
- Form inputs with subtle borders and focus states
- QR code displays with clean frames and instructions

**Data Display:**
- Booking status badges with color-coded states
- Wallet sections with clear balance typography
- Admin KPI cards with prominent numbers and trend indicators

**Overlays:**
- Booking confirmation modals with celebration micro-interactions
- Waiver acceptance with clear legal formatting
- Check-in/out scanner interface with camera overlay guides

## Visual Treatment & Marketing Pages

**Landing Page Structure (Max 4 sections):**
1. Hero with adventure imagery, clear value prop, and prominent "Join Adam's Club" CTA
2. Pricing cards section with plan comparison
3. Social proof with member testimonials and gear showcase
4. Footer with essential links and trust indicators

**Color Usage for Marketing:**
- Hero gradients: 142 69% 58% to 142 45% 35% (teal gradient overlay)
- Background treatments: Subtle nature-inspired patterns or clean geometric overlays
- Accent usage: Orange (25 95% 53%) sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- High contrast for outdoor imagery with text overlays

**Key Design Principles:**
- Adventure accessibility over extreme sports aesthetic
- Trust and safety emphasized through clean, professional layouts
- Mobile-first with generous touch targets
- Consistent outdoor lifestyle imagery showcasing gear in action
- Clear booking flow with progress indicators and confirmation states

## Images Section
- **Large Hero Image:** Yes - Adventure lifestyle shot showing people using rental gear (kayaking, biking, camping)
- **Gear Gallery:** High-quality product shots with consistent lighting and backgrounds
- **Experience Images:** Curated lifestyle photography showing members enjoying activities
- **Trust Indicators:** Clean member photos and testimonial imagery
- **Background Elements:** Subtle nature textures or geometric patterns that don't compete with content

The overall aesthetic balances outdoor adventure excitement with the reliability and professionalism required for a subscription service handling deposits and valuable equipment.
